Title: Car price analysis based on imported Japanese reconditioned cars in Bangladesh

Sources: https://gari-import.com.bd/stock-list

Objectives:

1. Total listings based on different brands and models of Japanese reconditioned cars
2. Price comparison across various models and grades
3. In-depth analysis based on different features like year, engine capacity, fuel type, mileage, color, and rating
4. Identification of trends in pricing based on vehicle age and condition
5. Assessment of the availability of specific features (e.g., leather seats, moonroof) across listings
6. Evaluation of price variations by region or dealer within Bangladesh
7. Determination of the most popular brands and models based on listing frequency
8. Analysis of price-to-mileage ratios to assess value for money
9. Investigation of seasonal or market-driven price fluctuations
10. Comparison of reconditioned car prices with new car prices in the Bangladesh market
